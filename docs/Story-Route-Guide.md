## 1. Faction Overview

The game is divided into three major routes, where **Haven Outpost** and **Doom Castle** are mutually exclusive:

| Route | Core NPCs | Ultimate Goal | Opposing Route |
|-------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| Haven Outpost | Miss V, Su Xiaohan, Lili, Mu Miaomiao, Leader | Dawn Captain Badge / Harvest Goddess Ending / Love-Hate Ending | Doom Castle |
| Doom Castle | The King, The Queen | Crown Prince ID Card / Love-Hate Ending | Haven Outpost |
| Neutral | Dr. Chen, Luluwei, Liu Ruyan, Ma San | Rocket Ending / New Force Ending / Dish on the Table Ending / Elopement Ending | None |

**Key Rules**:
- After joining Haven Outpost (obtaining the Dawn Badge), you cannot borrow money, work, or apply for noble status at the Castle Bank
- After obtaining a Castle ID Card, Outpost NPCs will refuse to interact with you
- However, the **Castle Pass** does not trigger faction exclusion, nor will it disappear if you hold the Dawn Captain Badge

## 2. Haven Outpost Route

### 2.1 Miss V Route

**Location**: Haven Outpost → Talk to Miss V

Miss V is the Outpost's strongest combat instructor. Her quest line centers on "trading combat supplies for weapons," with rewards progressing from low-tier to top-tier.

| Quest | Affinity Required | Submit Items | Reward |
|:-----:|:-----------------:|--------------|--------|
| V1 First Meeting | 0 | Any 1 food + 1 drink | Small Dagger |
| V2 Hemostasis Training | 30 | Tourniquet ×5 | Knuckle Knife |
| V3 Serum Quest | 60 | Anti-Infection Serum ×8 | Dragon Copy Sword |
| V4 Hard Trial | 90 | Field Medical Kit ×8 | P226 + 9×19mm×50 |
| V5 First Aid Supplies | 120 | Medical First Aid Kit ×6 + Anti-Infection Serum ×6 | UZI + 9×19mm×50 |
| V6 Ultimate Trial | 150 | 2 of each medicine | M4A1 + 5.56×45mm NATO×50 |

**V6 Required Medicine List** (2 of each):
Band-Aid, Tourniquet, Antibiotics, Anti-Infection Serum, Surgical Kit, Universal Injection, Medical First Aid Kit, Field Medical Kit

**Affinity Gain Methods**: Daily conversation +1, gift giving +1, quest completion

**Story Highlights**:
- V1: Cold opening — "Can't fight on an empty stomach. Give me food, I'll give you weapons."
- V3: Beginning to acknowledge — "You've earned this."
- V4: Genuine smile — "You're the toughest survivor I've ever seen."
- V6: Admits you've surpassed her — "You've surpassed me." She solemnly hands over the M4A1

### 2.4 Mu Miaomiao Route (⭐Core Story)

**Location**: Haven Outpost → Talk to Mu Miaomiao

This is the **most touching quest line** in the entire game, and the only path to Ending 9 "I'm No Harvest Goddess."

#### Prerequisite Chain

```
Mu Miaomiao Affinity 150 → Unlock Secret → Obtain Agriculture Master Badge
                                        ↓
                    Go to Watchtower → Wreath Tombstone → Inspect (requires badge)
                                        ↓
                                  Dig (requires equipped Shovel)
                                        ↓
                              Obtain Miaomiao's Diary
                                        ↓
                    Return to Mu Miaomiao → Unlock "Buried Past"
```

#### Step 1: Obtain the Agriculture Master Badge

**Condition**: Mu Miaomiao Affinity ≥ 150

Affinity gain methods:
- Buy seeds (1 Cigarette → 3 seeds, Affinity +small amount)
- Tend experimental field (once daily, 2 turns, Affinity +1~3, get 1 random seed)
- Daily conversation +1, gift giving +1

**Secret Story**:

> Mu Miaomiao leads you through the ruins, over a low wall, to a hidden valley — flowers of all colors gently swaying in the twilight. Sunflowers, lavender, daisies... they stubbornly grow in the cracks of the ruins.
>
> "Beautiful, isn't it?" She stands among the flowers, the sunset painting her profile golden.
>
> "There are still things worth protecting in this world."
>
> She picks a badge from the flowers and hands it to you: "My mentor left this for me. Now, it belongs to you."

**Reward**: Agriculture Master Badge

#### Step 2: Obtain Miaomiao's Diary

**Location**: Abandoned Mountaintop Watchtower → Select "Wreath Tombstone"

1. Select "Inspect":
   - Without badge: `This tombstone looks too eerie, you don't dare to inspect it.`
   - With badge: `I feel drawn to it somehow, these flower wreaths look familiar, and the dirt beside them seems to hide something.`

2. Select "Dig":
   - Without shovel equipped: `You need to equip a shovel, otherwise you can't dig.`
   - With shovel equipped: Obtain **Miaomiao's Diary**

**Diary Contents**:

> "Day 1. Mom and Dad say they're hungry, there's nothing left to eat at home."
> "Day 15. Dad stopped moving. Mom stopped too. I'm so scared."
> "Day 30. I can't cry anymore, I have to survive. The neighbor uncle taught me to grow vegetables."
> "Day 100. I grew my first potato! Dad, Mom, can you see it?"
>
> The last page has a hand-drawn picture — a little girl with braids standing in the middle of a field, Dad on the left, Mom on the right, the three of them holding hands, with a big sun drawn above. Below, written in crooked letters:
>
> "Our family, together forever."

#### Step 3: Quest Chain "Buried Past"

Return to Mu Miaomiao. While holding the diary, the hidden option "Buried Past" is automatically unlocked.

> Mu Miaomiao sees the diary in your hands, her eyes instantly welling up.
> "This is... my diary. I thought it was buried there forever."
> "There are some things... I've always wanted to do, but I can't do them alone. Will you help me?"

##### Quest 1: Let's Have a Picnic

**Requirements**: Carrot ×3 + Potato ×3

> You follow Mu Miaomiao to the clearing beside the watchtower. She sets up a simple stove, and while peeling potatoes, she says:
> "When I was little, Mom and Dad would take me on picnics. Dad always burned the potatoes, and Mom would laugh at him."
>
> She ladles a bowl and hands it to you, suddenly smiling:
> "Try it, this is my mom's flavor."
>
> As the sun sets, the two of you sit by the ruins, drinking hot soup.
> "Thank you. This is the best soup I've had since the apocalypse."

**Reward**: Affinity +20

##### Quest 2: Longing Across Time

**Requirements**: High-Proof Liquor ×5 + Rye ×10

> You follow Mu Miaomiao back to the wreath tombstone.
>
> She pours the liquor in front of the tombstone, then scatters the rye grains one by one onto the soil.
>
> "Dad, Mom... I grew it."
>
> She crouches down, drawing something in the dirt with her fingers:
> "Look, Mom and Dad, I've grown them. Carrots, potatoes, rye... you'll never go hungry again."
>
> The wind blows, the paper pages rustling, as if responding to her.
>
> Mu Miaomiao stands up, wipes her tears, and suddenly smiles:
> "They must have seen it."

**Reward**: Affinity +30

##### Quest 3: Expand the Experimental Field

**Requirements**: 1 of each of 15 different crops

15 crop list: Swiss Chard, Purslane, Chicory, Jerusalem Artichoke, Cassava, Sweet Potato, Potato, Turnip, Salsify, Carrot, Chive, Goji Berry, Sorrel, Rye, Pea

> You lay out all 15 crops in front of Mu Miaomiao. Her eyes light up.
>
> "I'm going to expand the experimental field threefold. No one will ever lose their family to hunger again."
>
> She takes out an elegant longbow from the tool shed, wheat ear patterns carved into the bow body:
> "This bow was left by my mentor. Protect this field, and protect yourself."

**Reward**: Affinity +50, **Wilderness Bow** + Arrow ×30

**Wilderness Bow** (52 dmg/52% crit/520 integrity/Arrows/Legendary) — Story-unique weapon, cannot be obtained from drop pool.

#### Ending Trigger

After completing Quest 3, **Ending 9 "I'm No Harvest Goddess" is immediately triggered** (see [All Endings Guide](#6-all-endings-guide)).

### 2.5 Outpost Leader Route (⭐Core Story)

**Location**: Haven Outpost → Talk to Outpost Leader

This is the **core main quest spanning two major factions**, connecting the voice recorder, Leader, and Queen storylines.

#### Prerequisite Chain

```
Dawn Captain Badge → Riverside Camp → Foul-Smelling Tent → Strange Voice Recorder
                                                                ↓
                                                        Give to Outpost Leader
                                                                ↓
                                                        Quest 1: Letter to Sister
                                                                ↓
                                                        Give to The Queen (Belated Apology)
                                                                ↓
                                                        Obtain The Queen's Reply
                                                                ↓
                                                        Quest 2: Drinking and Reading the Letter
                                                                ↓
                                                        Quest 3: Alcohol Poisoning
                                                                ↓
                                                        Obtain Token of Affection → Unlock Queen Route
```

#### Step 1: Obtain the Strange Voice Recorder

**Location**: Riverside Camp → Select "Foul-Smelling Tent"

- Without Dawn Captain Badge: `This tent looks too dangerous, better not go in.`
- With Dawn Captain Badge: `After leading Haven Outpost members in battle for so long, what haven't I seen? Let's go in and take a look.`

After entering, select "Search" to obtain the **Strange Voice Recorder**.

**Recording Contents** (the most critical narrative fragment in the entire game):

> 🎙️ Familiar male voice: "For the sake of our old friendship, you'd better stay away from that Outpost Leader. In the apocalypse, true order requires strict resource consolidation so everyone can survive."
>
> Female voice: "Why?"
>
> Familiar male voice: "Because he's too foolish! Only by consolidating resources can we maximize benefits and fight off the zombie hordes! So I need people to work for me!"
>
> Female voice: "What does a real estate developer know?"
>
> Familiar male voice: "I've seen his cold attitude with my own eyes! I did so much for the Outpost Leader's sister. She and I were clearly in love. But what did he do? He tore us apart, and wouldn't even let me go off on my own, saying something about putting the collective first."
>
> Female voice: "You can't force love, you're too selfish. I'm going back to camp."
>
> Female voice: "Report to the Leader, through spy exchange, I've collected unfavorable remarks from the target about your sister."
>
> Bang — a pistol shot is heard in the recording.
>
> Familiar male voice: "To hell with your Outpost! I'm crowning myself King! None of you Outpost people will have it good, even this woman betrayed me! Trusting that Leader who severs people's relationships!"

**Interpretation**: The "familiar male voice" in the recording is The King, and the "female voice" is an intelligence operative sent by the Outpost. The King, resentful after being rejected by the Leader and having his romance with the Leader's sister broken up, killed the operative and crowned himself King.

#### Step 2: Leader Quest Chain

##### Quest 1: About the Voice Recorder

**Condition**: Hold the Strange Voice Recorder, talk to Outpost Leader

> The Leader flies into a rage after listening: "That beast... he killed the person I sent. That girl, she was just an intelligence operative, she did nothing wrong..."
>
> "Three years ago, he took a liking to my sister. I opposed their relationship because I didn't trust him. Later he showed his true colors — he wanted to control the Outpost's resources, and after I refused, he held a grudge."
>
> "I drove him away, and broke my sister's heart too. She left, went to the Castle, and became the current Queen."
>
> The Leader takes out a yellowed letter: "I wrote this letter three years ago, but never had the courage to send it. Can you... deliver it to her?"

**Reward**: Letter to Sister, Affinity +10

##### Intermediate Step: Belated Apology

**Condition**: Hold the Letter to Sister, go to the Castle and talk to The Queen

> The Queen casually takes the letter, then her hand freezes when she sees the handwriting.
> "This is... my brother's handwriting."
>
> She reads it word by word, then holds the letter to her chest for a long time.
>
> "Three years. It took him three years to have someone deliver it."
>
> "I won't go back. I've gotten used to life here. The Castle may be hypocritical, but at least... I don't have to face those memories anymore."
>
> She writes a few lines: "Give this to him. Tell him I don't hate him. But some wounds, even time can't heal."

**Reward**: The Queen's Reply

##### Quest 2: Drinking and Reading the Letter

**Condition**: Hold The Queen's Reply + High-Proof Liquor ×5

> The Leader pours himself a glass first and downs it in one go. Then carefully opens the reply.
>
> "She says... she doesn't blame me. But she's gotten used to life at the Castle and doesn't want to come back."
>
> "Three years ago, I wrote her a letter. I said, brother was wrong, shouldn't have driven away the person you loved. I said, come back, the Outpost will always be your home."
>
> "But she didn't come back."
>
> You drink all night. He talks about many things from the past. At the end, he drunkenly murmurs the last line of the reply:
>
> "Brother, I don't hate you. But some wounds, even time can't heal."

**Reward**: Affinity +20

##### Quest 3: Alcohol Poisoning

**Condition**: Universal Injection ×3

> The Leader is covered in red rashes, his face ashen — alcohol poisoning.
>
> You inject the three Universal Injections one by one. His breathing gradually steadies.
>
> The Leader slowly opens his eyes and smiles bitterly:
> "Excuse the spectacle. The great Outpost Leader, almost done in by alcohol."
>
> He fishes out a small box from under his pillow:
> "Don't get the wrong idea, this isn't a token of affection for you. This was a gift from that person — The King — to my sister. He left it at the Outpost, never took it back."
>
> "Now it's more useful to you. Take it, as thanks. Not just for saving my life, but for... helping me do what I couldn't for three years."
>
> You open the box. Inside is an exquisite pendant with a tiny sunflower engraved on it.

**Reward**: 9×19mm×100 + **Token of Affection**, Affinity +30

## 3. Doom Castle Route

### 3.1 The King Route

**Location**: Doom Castle → Talk to The King

**Prerequisite**: Must first purchase a Noble ID Card (25 Cigarettes)

The King route is the "corruption of power" path, from helping cure the Queen to pursuing immortality to assassinating the Leader.

| Quest | Requirement | Reward | Rank |
|:-----:|-------------|--------|:----:|
| 1 The Queen's Troubles | Glucose Solution ×10 | Viscount ID Card | rank 2 |
| 2 The King's Banquet | High-Proof Liquor ×10 | Earl ID Card | rank 3 |
| 3 The King's Vices | Cigarettes ×10 + Flavor Beer ×5 + Power Beer ×5 | Marquis ID Card | rank 4 |
| 4 Immortality | Universal Injection ×3 | Duke ID Card | rank 5 |
| 5 Eliminate Dissent | Assassinate Outpost Leader | Crown Prince ID Card | rank 6 |

**⚠️ Critical Choice**: Quest 5 requires assassinating the Outpost Leader. The Leader has combat power equivalent to the Zombie King (HP 420, Damage 115, Evasion 25%). After successful assassination:
- The Leader dies, Outpost functions are restricted
- Can no longer interact with Miss V, Su Xiaohan, or Lili
- Cannot obtain the Dawn Captain Badge
- Triggers Ending 3 "Addiction to Power"

**Underground Key**: After reaching Crown Prince (rank 6), you can surrender an AWM sniper rifle to The King in exchange for the Underground Key. The underground area contains a Small Nuclear Generator, a key item for Dr. Chen's rocket chain.

### 3.2 The Queen Route (⭐Core Story)

**Location**: Inside Doom Castle → Talk to The Queen

The Queen route is the emotional conclusion of the voice recorder chain, and the only path to Ending 10 "Love and Hate."

#### Prerequisites

Hold the **Token of Affection** (from Outpost Leader Quest 3) → Talk to The Queen → Unlock "Talk About the Past"

> The Queen looks at the sunflower pendant with a complicated expression:
> "The one you're holding... it's from my brother's side, isn't it."
>
> She falls silent for a long time, then finally says softly:
> "What do you want to know?"

#### Quest Chain

##### Quest 1: Drown Your Sorrows

**Requirements**: Flavor Beer ×5 + Power Beer ×5

> The Queen picks up a bottle and takes a big gulp:
> "He made this pendant himself. He said sunflowers always face the sun, just like he always watches over me."
>
> "And then? Then he got power, got the Castle. But he still wanted me to wear this. Tell me, is this devotion, or a shackle?"

**Reward**: Royal Coins ×10

##### Quest 2: The King's Condition

**Requirements**: Goji Berry ×10

> "No matter what others say about him, he treats me... really well."
>
> "He has only one Queen. In this apocalypse, a King who stays faithful to one person, do you believe it?"

**Reward**: Royal Coins ×20

##### Quest 3: Sword Practice

**Requirements**: Western Sword ×3

> "He practices swordplay every day. He says only with a sword in hand can he protect the people he wants to protect."
>
> "But he protects everyone except himself. His body... is getting worse and worse."

**Reward**: Royal Coins ×30

##### Quest 4: Wedding Anniversary

**Requirements**: M700 ×1

> The Queen takes the M700, gently stroking the patterns on the stock:
> "He'll definitely love it."
>
> "You know what? He never lets me suffer the slightest grievance. In this apocalypse, he gave me a home, a crown, and... all of his sincerity."
>
> "But sincerity is something that sometimes hurts more than bullets."
>
> She turns to look at you, a smile of relief on her face:
> "Take good care of him for me. And take good care of... my brother for me too."

**Reward**: Royal Coins ×40 + **Castle Pass**

#### Ending Trigger

After completing Quest 4, **Ending 10 "Love and Hate" is immediately triggered** (see [All Endings Guide](#6-all-endings-guide)).

## 4. Neutral Route

### 4.1 Companion System

#### Liu Ruyan (Half-Infected Woman)

**Location**: Downtown Shopping Mall

**Discovery Text**: `You find a half-infected woman lying in the corner, she still has a trace of consciousness.`

**Rescue Condition**: Inject Anti-Infection Serum ×3 within 30 days

**Effect After Rescue**: Automatically gathers supplies daily (food/drinks/fruit/ammo)

> "My name is Liu Ruyan... If you don't mind, I'd like to follow you. I may not be able to help much, but I can help you gather some supplies..."

**⚠️ Not Rescued in Time**: The woman fully turns into a zombie, beyond saving.

**💡 Tip**: After discovering Liu Ruyan, a rescue countdown reminder is automatically added to your Survival Notes, helping you track the remaining days.

#### Luluwei (Nurse Zombie)

**Location**: Suburb Large Hospital

**Discovery Text**: `A female zombie in a tattered nurse uniform huddles in the corner, her badge reads "Luluwei." Her eyes still retain a trace of human sanity.`

**Recruitment Condition**: Feed canned food to raise Affinity to 150 (non-canned food doesn't increase Affinity), then select "Bring Home"

**Effect After Recruitment**: Automatically brings back 1 medicine item daily

> Luluwei follows you back to the survivor tent, she is now your loyal companion.

### 4.2 Dr. Chen Rocket Chain

**Location**: Biochemical Research Institute → Talk to Dr. Chen

This is the only path to 3 space endings, and the most complex quest chain in the game.

#### Prerequisite Chain

```
Dr. Chen Trade (30 Serum → M700) → Defeat Giant Puppet → Rocket Quest 1 (Small Nuclear Generator)
                                                    ↓
                                    Requires Castle Crown Prince status → Surrender AWM to The King → Underground Key
                                                    ↓
                                            Obtain Generator from Underground Area
                                                    ↓
                                    Rocket Quest 2 (Pure Energy ×30, 1 per day)
                                                    ↓
                                    Rocket Quest 3 (Medical ×10 + Food ×10 + Drink ×10)
                                                    ↓
                                            Rocket Repaired → Choose One of Three
```

#### Doctor's Initial Trade

**Requirement**: Anti-Infection Serum ×30

> The Doctor takes the 30 serums, hands trembling slightly: "Thank you so much... this is enough for me to complete my research!"

**Reward**: M700 + 7.62×51mm×30

#### Rocket Quest 1: Small Nuclear Generator

**Prerequisite**: Doctor trade completed + Defeat Giant Zombie Puppet (Space Base)

> "First, I need a Small Nuclear Generator. There's a batch of such equipment in the underground area of Doom Castle. But it's been sealed off by The King, you need his permission to enter."

**💡 Tip**: If prerequisites are not met, the game will display specific missing conditions in Dr. Chen's dialogue options (such as "Need to complete serum trade first" or "Need to defeat the Giant Puppet at the Space Base first").

**How to Obtain**: Castle Crown Prince (rank 6) → Surrender AWM to The King → Obtain Underground Key → Get it from Underground Area

#### Rocket Quest 2: Pure Energy ×15

**Source**: Research Institute Energy Extraction Well, can extract 1 per day (activates after completing Quest 1)

#### Rocket Quest 3: Survival Supplies

**Requirement**: Medical ×10 + Food ×10 + Drink ×10

#### Launch Choice

After the rocket is repaired, go to the Space Base Launch Center and choose one of three:

| Choice | Condition | Ending |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Board the rocket yourself | None | Ending 6: Into Space (Final) |
| Bring someone important | Miss V/Xiaohan/Lili Affinity all ≥150 | Ending 7: Spark of Hope (Final) |
| I won't go, Doctor, you go | None | Ending 8: Stay Behind (Continuable) |

**💡 Affinity Progress**: When selecting "Bring someone important," the game displays the current Affinity progress for Miss V, Su Xiaohan, and Lili (e.g., V:80/150 Xiaohan:60/150 Lili:150/150), making it easy to see how much more you need.

## 9. Recommended Route Planning

### Route A: Full Story Experience (Recommended for First Playthrough)

This route allows you to experience the most story content in a single playthrough, achieving Endings 9 and 10.

```
Phase 1 (Early Game): Outpost Foundation Building
├─ Join Haven Outpost
├─ Advance Miss V route to V4 (Affinity 90)
├─ Advance Su Xiaohan route to xh3 (Affinity 90)
├─ Raise Lili Affinity (use recycle/repair services)
├─ Raise Mu Miaomiao Affinity (buy seeds + tend experimental field)
└─ Rescue Liu Ruyan (Mall, 3 serums within 30 days)

Phase 2 (Mid Game): Agriculture Line + Companions
├─ Mu Miaomiao Affinity 150 → Agriculture Master Badge
├─ Watchtower Tombstone Dig → Miaomiao's Diary
├─ Mu Miaomiao Quests 1-3 → Wilderness Bow → Ending 9
├─ Rescue Luluwei (Hospital, feed canned food)
├─ Push Miss V/Xiaohan/Lili Affinity to 150
└─ Raise Leader Affinity

Phase 3 (Late Game): Voice Recorder Chain + Queen Route
├─ All NPC Affinity 150 → Dawn Captain Badge
├─ Riverside Camp → Foul-Smelling Tent → Strange Voice Recorder
├─ Leader Quests 1-3 → Token of Affection
├─ Queen Quests 1-4 → Castle Pass → Ending 10
└─ At this point 2 endings achieved, can continue playing

Phase 4 (End Game): Rocket Line (Optional)
├─ Doctor trade (30 serums)
├─ Defeat Giant Puppet
├─ ⚠️ This step requires Castle identity, mutually exclusive with Captain Badge
└─ If you want to experience rocket endings, need to choose Castle route in a new playthrough
```
