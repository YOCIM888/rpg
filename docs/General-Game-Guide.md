## I. Basic Mechanics Overview

### Time System

One day = **8 turns** (Dawn → Morning → Forenoon → Noon → Afternoon → Dusk → Night → Late Night)

### Core Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Penalty at Zero / Cap |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| ❤️ HP | Max 240 | Reaching 0 = Death (Ending 1) |
| 🍖 Satiety | -4 per turn | Reaching 0 → -10 HP per turn |
| 💧 Hydration | -4 per turn | Reaching 0 → -10 HP per turn |
| 🦠 Infection Value | +2 when hit by zombie | Reaching 100 = Death (Ending 1) |
| 💔 Crash Value | +20 automatically every 16 awake turns | ≥50 shows "Crashed"; ≥100 = unable to fight/eat/drink/sleep |

### Combat Parameters

| Parameter | Value |
|:-----|:-----|
| Flee success rate | 25% |
| Ranged dodge rate | 60% (dodge zombie attacks in ranged combat mode) |
| Critical hit multiplier | 2× |
| Liu Ruyan coordinated attack | 30% chance, deals 20 extra damage |
| Luluwei post-battle healing | Automatically +20 HP after winning a battle |
| Infection Value (when hit) | +2 per hit |

### Sleep

- Consumes **4 turns**
- Restores HP **+20–40**
- Reduces Crash **-10–25**
- Base level bonus: +0 / +5 / +10 / +15 / +20 HP

### Must-Know for Beginners

- **Haven Outpost is monster-free and allows infinite survival** — sleep, eat, drink, and work here
- **Barn yields pickable fruit every 3 days**, and the farmhouse has abandoned vegetable fields
- **Climbing the watchtower reduces Crash**
- **Enjoying the view at the riverside port also reduces Crash**
- **You cannot go outside during acid rain** — plan ahead

## II. Quick Start: The Wage-Slave Survival Route

> For: Players who don't want to take risks and prefer steady development
> Difficulty: ★☆☆☆☆ (Basically impossible to die)

### Core Loop (8 turns per day)

```
Work(1 turn) → Work(2 turns) → Work(3 turns) → Work(4 turns)
→ Beg/Chat(5th turn) → Eat & Drink(6th turn) → Sleep(7-8th turns)
```

### Cost-Benefit Analysis

| Item | Cost | Recovery Method |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| Crash +40 (from working) | Sleep recovery | One sleep = -10–25 Crash |
| Hydration -18 (from working) | Drink water | Begging / Outpost scavenging / Warehouse |
| Satiety -18 (from working) | Eat food | Barn fruit every 3 days, Outpost food |

**Conclusion:** The resources earned from working far exceed the costs. As long as you don't go out fighting, **you will never die**.

### Work Route Map

1. After waking up each day, **beg** first (talking to Outpost NPCs has a chance to give fruit)
2. **Work** (Note: You cannot work at night)
3. Use earned resources to **eat and drink**
4. **Sleep** through the night to restore Crash and HP
5. Go to the **Barn to pick fruit** every 3 days
6. Save up enough strength before exploring further

## III. Full Walkthrough: From Rookie to Veteran

### Stage 1: Early Survival (Days 1–5)

**Goal:** Stay alive + Get a basic weapon

#### Day 1

1. ✅ **Go to Haven Outpost and talk to Miss V**, accept the first meeting quest (1 food + 1 drink)
2. ✅ Complete the quest to get a **Small Dagger** — your first proper weapon
3. ✅ Go to the **Rural Abandoned Barn** (only 5% encounter rate) to scavenge for food and weapons
4. ✅ Watch your HP — if you're feeling unwell, go home and sleep

#### Days 2–5

- 🔄 Daily loop: Work → Eat → Sleep
- 🏪 Go to the Barn to **pick fruit** every 3 days (stable food source)
- 🎒 Go to the **Riverside Campground** (12% encounter rate) to scavenge for backpacks, aiming for a **28-slot hiking backpack** or better
- ⚔️ The Barn has a chance to drop Melee Weapons — swap out the Small Dagger once you find an **Iron Shovel / Crowbar**

#### Weapon Progression Route

```
Fists(10) → Small Dagger(40) → Iron Shovel(48)/Crowbar(60)/Fire Axe(68)
→ Knuckle Knife(66)/Machete(75) → Rapier(79)/Dragon Sword(88)/Long Lance(90)
→ Katana(99)/Tang Blade(102)/Chainsaw(105) → Universal Sword(118)
```

### Stage 2: Accumulation Phase (Days 5–15)

**Goal:** Stockpile resources, upgrade gear, prepare to rescue companions

#### Recommended Map Progression

| Map | Encounter Rate | Main Loot |
|:-----|:-----:|:-----|
| Rural Abandoned Barn | 5% | Food + Weapons |
| Mountain Farmhouse Village | 10% | Food + Fruit + Seeds |
| Riverside Campground | 12% | Backpacks + Medicine |
| University Campus | 15% | Food + Backpacks |
| Suburban Abandoned Gas Station | 18% | Ammo + Weapons |

#### Key Actions

1. 🎒 **Find a large backpack**: Scavenge bodies at the Campground, Watchtower (drops 64-slot ultra-heavy load backpack)
2. ⚔️ **Farm weapons**: Gas Station, Industrial Park (30% melee rate) — **Industrial Park is the holy grail for farming Melee Weapons!**
3. 💊 **Stockpile medicine**: Focus on **Anti-Infection Serum** and **Tourniquets** (needed in large quantities for later quests)
4. 🚬 **Save Cigarettes**: Cigarettes are hard currency! Lili recycles Melee Weapons for Cigarettes, and the Black Market Trader accepts Cigarettes for everything

#### What If You Can't Win a Fight?

- If your HP is low, **go home and sleep** — don't push it
- If Crash is high, go to the **Watchtower and climb** (reduces Crash) or **enjoy the view at the riverside port**
- Bring enough food and water before heading out

### Stage 3: Companion Recruitment (Days 15–30)

**Goal:** Rescue Liu Ruyan + Recruit Luluwei within 30 days

>- ⚠️ **Liu Ruyan has a time limit: After 30 days, she will fully turn into a zombie and can no longer be saved!**
- 💡 After discovering Liu Ruyan, a rescue countdown reminder will be automatically added to your Survival Notes

#### Prerequisite: Get a 60+ Damage Weapon

- Go to the **Barn / Gas Station / Industrial Park** to farm weapons
- Or do quests for Miss V to get good weapons

#### Rescue Liu Ruyan (Time-Limited!)

```
Downtown Shopping Mall (35% encounter rate)
  → Find the half-infected woman (Liu Ruyan)
  → Need 3 Anti-Infection Serums to save her
  → Successfully save her → Trigger long story event → Bring her home → Get your first companion
```

**How to Get Serum:**

| Method | Description |
|:-----|:-----|
| 🏥 Suburban Large Hospital (50% encounter rate) | 40% chance to drop medicine — farm as fast as possible |
| 🛒 Black Market Trader | Random spawn, occasionally has serum |
| 💊 Scavenging mid-to-high difficulty maps | Police Station, Research Institute, etc. |

**Hospital Farming Tips:**

- Don't be greedy — do one run then go home and sleep, come back after waking
- Bring enough food and water
- Go with a 60+ damage weapon, otherwise you can't beat the Military Police Zombies (160 HP / 36 damage)

#### Liu Ruyan Quest Chain (Unlocked After Rescue)

After rescuing Liu Ruyan, go to **University Campus → An Old Classroom** to start Liu Ruyan's exclusive quest chain, consisting of 4 quest segments, which can ultimately trigger **Ending 12 "Elopement"**. See the [Liu Ruyan University Quest Chain](#liu-ruyan-university-quest-chain) section for details.

#### Recruit Luluwei (No Time Limit)

```
Suburban Large Hospital (50% encounter rate)
  → Find the sane nurse zombie (Luluwei)
  → Feed her canned food (Peach/Bean/Beef/Military Ration/Sardine/Tuna/Saury/Mackerel cans)
  → Affinity reaches 150 → Bring her home → Get your second companion
```

**Tip:** Canned food can be farmed at the Barn, Supermarket, and Farmhouse. Each feeding gives +2–3 Affinity, so you'll need roughly 50–75 cans.

#### Companion Benefits

| Companion | Requirement | Daily Output / Combat Effect |
|:----:|:-----|:-----|
| 🌸 Liu Ruyan | 3 Serums (30-day limit) | 2 random supplies daily (Food / Drink / Fruit / Ammo) |
| 🏥 Luluwei | 150 Affinity (no time limit) | 1 medicine daily (Band-Aid ~ Epinephrine) |
| ⚔️ Liu Ruyan (Combat) | Recruited | 30% chance of coordinated attack in combat, dealing 20 extra damage |
| 💉 Luluwei (Post-Combat) | Recruited | Automatically heals +20 HP after winning a battle |

#### Boss Strategy Tips

**Ma San (Highway Service Area):**
- Go with a 70+ Melee Weapon or a handgun
- Bring plenty of medicine — his ranged first strike can cripple you
- Defeating him gives you the AK47, instantly propelling you into the firearm era

**Shadow (Warehouse Supermarket):**
- Even harder than Ma San! Recommended to have the Dragon Sword or a firearm before attempting
- 50% dodge rate, so ranged attacks have low hit rates
- Defeating him gives the GP100, a powerful revolver

**Zombie King (Zombie Nest):**
- **The strongest zombie enemy in the game, bar none**
- 115 damage — two hits can kill a full-HP player
- Recommended to bring full Field Medical Kits + full-durability Melee Weapon + Ranged Weapon
- Recommended loadout: M4A1 + ample 5.56 ammo + Universal Sword / Chainsaw
- Defeating him gives the Silence Badge, a key condition for triggering the "Become a New Force" ending

**Bank Manager (Doom Castle):**
- Only triggers combat if you default on your loan
- 350 HP / 30% dodge, moderate difficulty
- Killing him clears your debt + gives the Katana (99 dmg / epic)

#### Research Institute Ultimate Trade: Dr. Chen

```
Bio-Research Institute (70% encounter rate)
  → Turn in 30 Anti-Infection Serums
  → Get M700 Sniper Rifle(120 dmg) + 7.62×51mm ×30
```

**The M700 is one of the strongest obtainable Ranged Weapons in the game**, second only to the AWM (200 dmg).

**Dr. Chen Rocket Quest Chain Prerequisites:** You must first complete the Doctor's serum trade + defeat the Giant Zombie Puppet at the Space Base. 💡 If prerequisites are not met, the game will display the specific missing conditions in the Doctor's dialogue options.

#### Endgame Endings

See the [Ending System](#ending-system) section below. After completing all main quests, you can trigger the "Become a New Force" ending and obtain the ultimate Melee Weapon **Blade of Fearlessness(130 dmg / 20% combo / legendary)**.

## VII. Advanced Tips

### 💰 Cigarette Economy

**Cigarettes are the doomsday currency!** All high-level trades require Cigarettes.

#### Core Loop: Cigarettes → Supplies → Cigarettes

```
Scavenge weapons → Lili recycles → Get Cigarettes
    ↓
Cigarettes → Black Market Trader → Buy ammo / weapons / seeds
    ↓
Better weapons → Challenge high-risk maps → More weapons / supplies
    ↓
Extra weapons → Lili recycles → More Cigarettes
    ↑_________________________|
```

#### Cigarette Acquisition Summary

| Method | Yield | Stability |
|:-----|:-----|:-----:|
| Lili recycles Melee Weapons | 1 Cigarette / weapon | ⭐⭐⭐ Late-game weapon overflow, stable cash-out |
| Lili recycles Ranged Weapons | 2 Cigarettes / weapon | ⭐⭐⭐ Ranged weapons are worth more |
| Scavenge Industrial Park / Factory Warehouse | 10% Cigarette drop | ⭐⭐ Low probability |
| Kill Black Market Trader | 2–5 Cigarettes | ⭐ Risky |
| Castle work | 1 Cigarette / shift | ⭐ High cost (18 Satiety + 18 Hydration + 40 Crash) |
| Castle audience (Nobility rank 5+) | 2 Cigarettes / day | ⭐⭐⭐ Stable passive income |
| Lili 150 Affinity reward | 20 Cigarettes (one-time) | ⭐⭐⭐ One-time lump sum |

#### Cigarette Spending Priority

1. **Clear Infection (10 Cigarettes)** — Must clear when Infection Value nears 100, otherwise death
2. **Repair Firearms (10 Cigarettes)** — Dangerous when your main weapon breaks
3. **Buy ammo from Black Market (1 Cigarette / 3–6 rounds)** — Maintain ranged firepower
4. **Buy weapons from Black Market (10–15 Cigarettes)** — Upgrade gear
5. **Repair Bow/Crossbow (5 Cigarettes)** — Cheaper than guns, for daily mob clearing
6. **Buy seeds (1 Cigarette / pack)** — Base farming has long-term returns

**Money-Saving Tip:** Use bows/crossbows for small mobs (zero repair cost), only use firearms for Bosses / strong enemies to maximize Cigarette efficiency.

## X. FAQ

**Q: How do I start the game?**
A: Scavenge at the Barn (safe) → Get the Small Dagger from Miss V → Work to save up → Gradually explore low-risk maps.

**Q: What if I failed to save Liu Ruyan?**
A: After 30 days, she will fully turn into a zombie. You can only reload a save or start over. So **make sure to gather 3 Serums within 30 days**.

**Q: What if my backpack is full?**
A: Build a warehouse (Base Building → Build Warehouse) and store unused supplies there. Warehouse max is 100 slots.

**Q: How do I quickly farm Affinity?**
A: Talk 4 times per day (+1 Affinity each), give gifts (Food / Drink +3, Medicine +5, Cigarettes +2). Lili has higher gift bonuses (Food +6, Medicine +10).

**Q: What if my Infection Value is high?**
A: Use Wound Cleanser(-25) / Antibiotics(-25) / Anti-Infection Serum(-60) / Improved Antibody Injection(-90), or pay Lili 10 Cigarettes to clear -50 Infection. High Castle nobility rank also provides Infection treatment rates.

**Q: What if my Crash Value is high?**
A: Sleep(-10–25), Painkiller(-5) / Epinephrine(-10) / Surgical Kit(-10) / Field Medical Kit(-30) / Universal Injection(-50), Watchtower climb(-10), River view(-10), Castle Ball(-50), Bitter Beer(-10) / Power Beer(-10) / High-Proof Liquor(-30).

**Q: What if my Melee Weapon breaks?**
A: Lili only repairs **Ranged Weapons**. If a Melee Weapon breaks, just find a new one. Fortunately, Melee Weapons drop frequently.

**Q: What if my Ranged Weapon's integrity is low?**
A: Pay Lili 10 Cigarettes to repair (firearms) or 5 Cigarettes to repair (bow/crossbow). Bows/Crossbows **do not consume integrity** — use bows for small mobs to save repair costs.

**Q: What's the strongest weapon?**
A: For melee, it's the Imperial Sword (250 dmg / 1 durability / legendary, shatters after one hit) and the Blade of Fearlessness (130 dmg / 100 durability / 20% combo / legendary, practically the strongest); for ranged, it's the AWM (200 dmg / legendary). The Blade of Fearlessness is obtained through the "Become a New Force" ending. There are also story-exclusive weapons: Wilderness Bow (52 dmg / 52% crit / legendary), Royal Short Gun (99 dmg / legendary), Royal Silver Sword (88 dmg / 188 durability / 20% combo / legendary).

**Q: How many endings does the game have?**
A: The game has **12 endings**: Death (Game Over), Unknown Future (999 days), Addiction to Power (Crown Prince Identity Card), Shine Bright (Haven Captain Badge), Become a New Force (6 conditions), Into Space (Solo rocket / Game Over), Spark of Hope (Team rocket / Game Over), Stay Behind (Dr. Chen leaves), I Am No Farm God (Mu Miaomiao Quest 3), Love and Hate (The Queen Quest 4), Become the Dish (Ma San Quest 4 Accept / Game Over), Elopement (Liu Ruyan Quest 4 / Game Over). Endings 2–5 and 8–10 allow continued play.

**Q: How do I get the AWM?**
A: The AWM (200 dmg / legendary) is the strongest Ranged Weapon in the game. It can only be obtained by finding the crashed Space Capsule at the **Top-Secret Space Base** with a 5% chance (one-time only). The Space Base has a 90% encounter rate — bring full medicine and top-tier gear. Extremely difficult to obtain in normal gameplay; it's an ultimate collector's item.

**Q: How do I join Haven Outpost?**
A: Find the **Outpost Leader** at Haven Outpost and select "Join Outpost" to get the **Haven Badge**. Note: You cannot join while holding a Noble Identity Card (the Leader will refuse) — you must cancel your Noble Identity first.

**Q: What does Noble Identity do?**
A: The Noble Identity Card (25 Cigarettes) lets you enter the **Doom Castle Interior** and enjoy these privileges: Attend Noble Banquet (Satiety+50 / Hydration+50), Attend Noble Ball (Crash-50), Rest in Noble Guest Room (HP+50). Higher nobility ranks also provide Infection treatment (up to 80%), daily audience 2 Cigarettes, Garden random medicine, etc. However, holding Noble Identity removes Haven Outpost NPC interaction privileges and prevents joining Haven Outpost.

**Q: What happens if I default on my Castle loan?**
A: Defaulting (over 10 days unpaid) triggers **confiscation of all items** — everything on your person is stripped! This penalty triggers only once; after repaying the debt, things return to normal. Make sure you can repay within 10 days before borrowing, or ask the Bank Manager for a 5-day grace period before going overdue.

**Q: How do I handle dual identity conflicts?**
A: The game **does not allow holding both the Haven Badge and Noble Identity Card simultaneously**. If you have the Haven Badge and then register for Noble Identity, the system automatically removes the Noble Identity Card and keeps the Haven Badge. If you have a Noble Identity Card and try to join Haven Outpost, the Outpost Leader will refuse interaction. Decide on your route before choosing an identity.

**Q: How do I get Royal Coins?**
A: Complete The Queen quest line (requires first completing the Outpost Leader quest chain to get the Love Token) for Royal Coin rewards. After completing Quest 4, "Royal Trade" is unlocked, allowing you to exchange Goji Berries (2→1 coin) and Rapiers (1→5 coins) for Royal Coins.

**Q: What does the Castle Pass do?**
A: The Castle Pass (The Queen Quest 4 reward) lets you pass through guards and enter the Castle directly, without an Identity Card or bribe. It **does not** trigger Haven Outpost排斥, and it won't disappear even if you hold the Haven Captain Badge.

**Q: How do I get Miaomiao's Diary?**
A: Go to the Mountain Abandoned Watchtower, select "Wreath Gravestone" → "Examine" (requires Farming Master Badge) → "Dig" (requires equipping Iron Shovel) to obtain Miaomiao's Diary. After obtaining the diary, talk to Mu Miaomiao to unlock the hidden quest line.

**Q: How much Infection Value do I gain each time a zombie hits me?**
A: +2 Infection Value per zombie hit. Infection Value reaching 100 means death — be sure to treat it promptly with Wound Cleanser / Serum / Antibody Injection.

**Q: What happens when Crash Value reaches 100?**
A: Crash Value reaching 50 shows a "Crashed" status, and reaching **100 makes you unable to fight, eat, drink, or sleep**. Every 16 awake turns automatically adds +20 Crash — remember to sleep regularly or use Crash-reducing items. 💡 When Crash is full, the game will prompt you to use Painkiller (Crash-5), Epinephrine (Crash-10), Surgical Kit (Crash-10), Field Medical Kit (Crash-30), or Universal Injection (Crash-50) to restore your mental state. Normal functions resume once Crash drops below 100.

**Q: Do bows/crossbows really not consume integrity?**
A: Yes! Bows and crossbows **do not consume integrity**, but they have crit rates (not 0%), making them ideal for daily mob clearing to save repair costs. Firearms lose -1 integrity per shot and need regular repairs from Lili.

**Q: How do I unlock Ma San's quest?**
A: First eat "mystery meat" at the National Highway Service Area Restaurant (⚠️ +10 Infection Value each time), then talk to Ma San and select "About the restaurant food." Note: Ma San Quest 4, whether you accept or defeat Ma San, triggers Ending 11 "Become the Dish" (Endgame) — this is an irreversible choice.

**Q: How do I get Gasoline?**
A: Find Wang Tiezhu at the Suburban Abandoned Gas Station — 5 Cigarettes for 1 Gasoline. You can also use the cheat code `/get_gasoline_汽油_5` (get 5 each time).

**Q: How do I start Liu Ruyan's quest line?**
A: First rescue Liu Ruyan (3 Serums at the Shopping Mall), then go to "An Old Classroom" at the University Campus.

**Q: How do I get to the Small Island?**
A: You need a Castle Identity Card + 1 Gasoline (Gasoline can be exchanged via Wang Tiezhu using Cigarettes), then take the Castle Yacht from the Riverside Port Dock.

**Q: How do I borrow from The Island Bank?**
A: Requires Castle Pass or Castle Identity Card. You can borrow 10 / 20 / 30 Royal Coins, 30-day repayment period, overdue penalty adds 10% interest.

**Q: What if my Crash Value is full and I encounter a zombie?**
A: At 100% Crash Value, you enter combat unable to attack and can only take hits passively. The game will display a ⚠ warning before combat. It's recommended to first use Painkillers/Epinephrine or other Crash-reducing medicine to restore your mental state before engaging in combat.

**Q: Can I choose the quantity when buying ammo from the Black Market Trader?**
A: Yes! When buying ammo, you can choose to spend 1, 5, 10, or all of your Cigarettes — 1 Cigarette ≈ 3–6 rounds of ammo (random). You no longer have to spend all your Cigarettes at once.

**Q: How do I track the New Force ending progress?**
A: After completing any prerequisite condition, a "New Force" progress entry will be automatically added to your Survival Notes, showing the completion status of all 6 conditions (X/6).

**Q: What are the requirements for the rocket's "Bring Important People" option?**
A: You need Miss V, Su Xiaohan, and Lili's Affinity all at 150. When selecting this option, the game will display current Affinity progress (e.g., V:80/150 Xiaohan:60/150 Lili:150/150), making it easy to see how much more you need.
